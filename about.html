About I.T.S.M.R.

OPEN TO GENERAL PUBLIC

The Institute for Type Safe Memetic Research is a joint project, created by One Happy Fellow and David Abram.

What is The Institute about? It's a place to write roughly SCP-flavoured prose, create elaborate quizes with original music and have type-safe fun. We welcome collabolators, check out The Institute's GitHub page.

We are forever thankful to Operative Goreng for a kick in the right direction and beautiful logo.
