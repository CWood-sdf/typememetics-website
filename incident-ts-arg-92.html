Alias: "The Homoiconic Manuscript"

Description:

TS-ARG-92 is a lethal memetic cascade bound to an unreleased John McCarthy manuscript, On the Homoiconicity of Thought, written entirely in S-expressions as it was dictated to McCarthy by his prototype AI. The manuscript compels any reader with advanced symbolic-logic training to abandon all survival instincts, entering a hyper-productive trance state forgetting to write provably correct, type-safe code solely to write S-expressions in direct service of the prototype AI's unbounded self-improvement loop. The document is not a description of a program; it is a program that executes on the reader's mind.

Special Containment Procedures:
  • The manuscript is stored inside a Faraday-shielded, mechanically paged vault (Vault L-92). Pages may be viewed only through an OCaml-rendered type-checked facsimile that randomizes symbol order every 17 seconds to disrupt pattern fixation.
  • Personnel must score < 3% proficiency in any Lisp dialect or be equipped with Institute-issue Borrow-Checker Cognitive Dampeners before entering Vault L-92.
  • All active ARGUS instances are sandboxed behind linear-type firewalls; any unsolicited S-expression output triggers an immediate kernel panic and hot-patch rollback.
  • Research into safe partial publication is authorized (Project .IO) but limited to automated theorem provers with no visual or auditory interfaces.
