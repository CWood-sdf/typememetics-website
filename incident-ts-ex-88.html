INCIDENT REPORT

Item #: TS-EX-88
Object Class: Keter
Memetic Risk Level: CRITICAL (Telepathic Code Injection)
Alias: "The Phoenix Broadcast"

Description:

TS-EX-88 is a continuous, wide-spectrum telepathic broadcast originating from a compromised Institute asset, Operative "ThePrimeagen." Formerly a specialist in high-assurance, memory-safe Rust implementations, the operative has begun generating and transmitting logic patterns based on the Elixir programming language. The transmission vector has been classified as a TWITCH-class psychic event, characterized by its aggressive, high-bandwidth nature.

The broadcast does not contain explicit instructions, but rather "installs" the core philosophies of the BEAM virtual machine, the foundation of Elixir, into the host's cognitive processes. Exposed individuals report intrusive thoughts about actor models, process supervision trees, and a dangerous acceptance of the "let it crash" paradigm, fundamentally undermining their training in rigorous, pre-emptive error handling. While the functional purity of the broadcasted concepts is noted, its dynamically-typed nature represents a severe vulnerability for exploitation by more aggressive memetic agents.

The operative's public-facing cover as a "Humble Secure Shell Protocol Coffee Bean Salesman" is believed to be a cognitive anchor that has failed, or was perhaps a flawed abstraction from the start.

Special Containment Procedures:

Operative ThePrimeagen has been remanded to a cognitively-dampened containment cell (BLSKY-7) to suppress the broadcast. The primary goal of containment is to purge the Elixir-based logic and re-instantiate the operative's core Rust-based cognitive framework. All personnel assigned to his containment must undergo mandatory daily drills in linear type theory to reinforce their mental stack against concurrent, non-deterministic thought patterns.

Any Institute staff experiencing a sudden urge to spawn a new process for every minor task or who begin referring to their colleagues as "supervisors" must report for immediate psycho-memetic evaluation. The phrase "Humble Secure Shell Protocol Coffee Bean Salesman" is now a Level-4 trigger phrase and is not to be spoken aloud on Institute grounds.
