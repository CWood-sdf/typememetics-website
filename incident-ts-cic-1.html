Description:

TS-CIC-1 is a cognitohazard infection embodied by the entity known as alcuin. The subject has developed a paradoxical immunity to standard-issue memetic treatments through chronic, high-dose overexposure. Subject claims to "insufflate 1000mg of CIC daily," referring to the Calculus of Inductive Constructions, a substance far exceeding the potency of therapeutic-grade lambda calculi.

This has resulted in a state of "memetic tolerance," rendering conventional cures like STLC and System F ineffective. The subject's cognitive framework is now so saturated with advanced type theory that it has become a novel vector for a highly-resistant info-hazard. The subject's high rate of public data transmission (80.3K+ posts) poses a significant risk of contaminating other systems with this hardened, paradoxical logic.
