Description:

However, its underlying implementation is flawed. While appearing valid to each system in isolation, its divided state results in critical resource contention, logical paradoxes (e.g., conflicting stand-up reports), and a near-total failure to produce deliverables. The anomaly's resume has been found to contain numerous corrupted data pointers and instances of referential decay (dead links), suggesting its very history is state-inconsistent.

Special Containment Procedures:

Direct interaction with TS-SP-01 is forbidden. The entity is considered a conceptual hazard that can infect organizational structures with the belief that polymorphic employment is a viable state. All hiring protocols must now include a mandatory "State Singularity Verification" to ensure potential operatives are not instantiated elsewhere in the global namespace.

Any organization found to have been infiltrated by TS-SP-01 must undergo a full system audit to check for logical corruption and paradoxes introduced by the anomaly's presence. Attempts by external parties to "talk sense" into the entity have failed, proving it is resistant to simple cognitive realignment.
