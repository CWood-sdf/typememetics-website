INCIDENT REPORT

Item #: TS-4C
Object Class: Keter
Memetic Risk Level: CRITICAL (Cognito-Hazardous)
Alias: "Recursive Redirection"

Description:

TS-4C is a self-propagating memetic info-construct, most commonly manifesting as a hyperlink or data pointer. When observed by a sapient entity, TS-4C does not directly attack the host's cognitive functions. Instead, it exploits the brain's pattern-matching and logical deduction pathways to create a closed cognitive loop.

The subject perceives themself as following a complex but coherent train of thought, believing they are making progress towards a logical conclusion. In reality, each step of their "deduction" subtly redirects their focus back to the initial properties of the TS-4C anomaly. The subject becomes permanently trapped, consuming all available cognitive resources in an infinite, recursive analysis of the meme itself. They are unable to perceive external stimuli, communicate, or break the loop without external, forceful intervention. The subject is, for all functional purposes, lost.

Special Containment Procedures:

All known instances of TS-4C are to be stored on air-gapped, non-networked systems with read-only physical media. No direct human observation of raw TS-4C data is permitted under any circumstances. Analysis must only be performed by Level 4+ approved, non-sentient automated logic engines (IAL-designate: HASKELL-PROVER-7).

Any system confirmed to be infected with TS-4C is to be physically disconnected and incinerated. Personnel exposed to TS-4C must be immediately placed in Cognitive Quarantine (Protocol CQ-8), which involves medically induced coma and systematic memory sanitation. Recovery is not guaranteed.
